What is Adalytic?

Adalytic is a digital-advertising platform that enables everyday consumer Drivers to showcase brands they love and trust and get paid for doing it!

Ads are displayed on an removable, exterior LED monitor positioned on the roof of the vehicle. A matching algorithm selects ads for display based on the vehicle's real-time location.

Drivers earn money with no change to their daily driving routine. And Advertisers can optimize their budget, reaching target audiences that matter most.
